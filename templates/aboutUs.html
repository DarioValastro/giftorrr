Mission

The project aims to help everyone to make good gift! Gifts have to do with people happiness and joy, this is a crucial thing that could characterize every moment of our life. It could be a big present or simply a little thought, but what matters is to make that moment special, happiness is precious and Giftor it is thought to make that moment of happiness unmemorable!

Vision

Behind the choice of the gift you can hide many things that can make this small choice an important thing:

  • Choosing a gift suitable for the recipient will avoid waste of various kinds, for example production or money.
  • Choosing a sustainable gift could have an important meaning for the Earth, but above all an important impact.
  • Choosing a gift of a local product will support sellers in this particularly delicate historical context.
  • Choosing Giftor is making the right choice.

Values

Projected towards the future and interested in research and development, the promotion of eco-sustainable gift is a cutting-edge choice that we can make to make those who receive them happy and to concretely contribute to the well-being of the Planet on which we live.
